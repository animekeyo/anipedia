Personal Information We Collect

While using Anime Keyo, we may collect information from you as shown below: When registering an account on Anime Keyo and using its services, we may collect some information. The information we collect may include but not be limited to email address username, full name, age, messages, images or any other content by sending a message.

Information We May Inspect

To ensure users are complying with the TOS, we may view the contents of your private/public channels (Message logs). We may give you a warning followed by an account/server termination if you continue to not comply.

Data We Collect Automatically

While using Anime Keyo and its services, we will store certain information such as your IP Address, your account creation date and your activities such as your online status. We may store this information in the database owned by service providers. We may also track and store some details while you use Anime Keyo. This includes tracking the number of visitors on the website and the number of messages that a user has sent. We use IP Addresses to rate limit or ban users that may attack Anime Keyo.

Third-Party Services

You may permit us to connect your account with third party services such as your Google ,Discord or Facebook account. When you do this, it allows users to gain functionality (For example, Display Name, Email, Avatar). After you connect your account with third-party services, we may collect refresh tokens or some user details from the services and save them in the database. We use third party storage solution to store media you upload to Anime Keyo (Firebase).

Cookies

We use cookies and similar storage technologies to store local data such as recent emojis, logged in users id and other information. We may store and expand the usage of cookies to save more data as this service gets updated. We may use third-party technologies such as hCaptcha that use cookies store some data and also collect some data (See hCaptcha policies)
